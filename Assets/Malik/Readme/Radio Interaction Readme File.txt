Radio Interaction

Instructions:

Grab the ball using the grab button on the controller, and throw it, by releasing the grab button, at the play button to play music. 
Throw the ball again at the play button to select another song.

Grab the ball using the grab button on the controller, and throw it, by releasing the grab button, at the stop button to stop the music from playing.

Teleport by pushing the joystick forward and releasing it when you have selected where you want to teleport. Areas in which players can teleport
would have a circle displayed on the floor. Areas that players cannot teleport would not have a circle displayed on the floor.
